# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is designed to take you on a journey from fundamental concepts to advanced applications in the field of robotics, with a particular focus on the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift in how we approach robotics and artificial intelligence. Rather than treating AI as purely software-based intelligence, Physical AI emphasizes the importance of embodied cognition â€“ the idea that intelligence emerges from the interaction between an agent and its physical environment.

## What are Humanoid Robots?

Humanoid robots are robots that are designed to resemble and mimic human behavior. They typically have a head, torso, two arms, and two legs, and they can perform tasks similar to humans. The development of humanoid robots involves complex interdisciplinary research combining robotics, artificial intelligence, biomechanics, and cognitive science.
